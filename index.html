About me:

Computer junkie born in Alaska. In 6th grade I was introduced to the world of creating websites and wrote my first "Hello World" in HTML. I spent way too much time scouring the internet looking browser-based games. As a teenager I explored interests in IT, photography, music, theatre, and graphic design, then after concluding an enlistment in the military I pursued my love of both community arts and multimedia technology. Eventually I decided a career in web sounded pretty cool.
